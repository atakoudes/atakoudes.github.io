Worked 3 years as a Snowmaker at the Dartmouth Skiway and Middlebury Snow Bowl. Overnight shifts included operating snowmobiles, heavy machinery, maintaining good snow quality, and firing up/shutting down the system.

Field Research Assistant

Worked as a field assistant for Professor Jeff Munroe in the High Uintas Mountains of Utah. Fieldwork consisted of testing for internal ice within the largest rock glaciers in Utah. Ground Penetrating Radar was used to visualize the layers of the rock glacier and possibly find an ice layer. Another component of the project was hammering survey points into large boulders and using differential gps to mark their location with an error of +-1cm. These locations will be measured each summer to track the movement of the rock glaciers.
